International Relations Lenses

Realism in a Nutshell

  • States are the most important actors
  • States are rational and self-interested
  • States are in a state of anarchy

Liberalism in a Nutshell

  • States are important actors, but not the only ones
  • States/International Organizations are complex
  • Increased Complexity keeps peace through working together

Constructivism in a Nutshell

  • States, Organizations, and Ideas
  • Everything is a construct of Society
  • There are no facts only what society agrees upon
  • Takes ideas to change what constructs are set and takes time
